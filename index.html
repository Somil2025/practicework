About Me

Creativity isn’t just what I do — it’s how I live. From designing stickers that spark joy, to rapping verses that tell stories, to hosting art workshops that bring people together — every project reflects my passion and vision.

At 19, I’ve already explored many creative paths: founding my brand Beyond Sketching, selling art at local stalls, releasing music on Spotify, and editing visuals that stand out. Every piece of work I create carries a part of me — raw, purposeful, and authentic.

My biggest drive isn’t just art — it’s freedom. The freedom to create, to explore, to live on my own terms, and to one day give my family the life they deserve.

Through this website, I share everything I’m building — from sticker drops to rap projects, from creative services to workshops. If you believe in bold ideas and authentic expression, you’re in the right place. Let’s collaborate and make something unforgettable.
